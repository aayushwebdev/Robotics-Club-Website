QuadCopter
About DeviceThis is fully autonomous quad copter works based on the concept of image processing and terrain mapping with the help of high pixel camera module,LiDAR module,LiDAR censor.It's custom build flight controller controls the actuators and stabilizer the drone while in operation.Raspberry Pi works as flight computer and performance image processing and sends the data received from sensors through the flight controller.It is capable of reading Aruco codes and color detection throught Open CV preinstalled in it.The developed drone is also capable Mission planning.


Application/Utility:

*It can be used for remote sensing, mapping,surveying,operational oversight,and traffic control.
*pick and drop autonomous drone.
